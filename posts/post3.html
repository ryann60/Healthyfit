What Is the Mediterranean Diet?

So, what is a Mediterranean diet, exactly?

The Mediterranean diet is a way of eating inspired by traditional Mediterranean cuisine from countries such as Greece, Italy, Spain, and southern France. Instead of strict rules or calorie counting, it emphasizes whole, minimally processed foods and balanced meals.

At its core, the Mediterranean diet focuses on:

  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Legumes and nuts
  • Olive oil as the primary fat
  • Fish and seafood
  • Moderate dairy and poultry
  • Limited red meat and sweets

In short, it‚Äôs about enjoying real Mediterranean food that nourishes your body and tastes great.

Yes‚ÄîMediterranean diet for weight loss works, and it works well.

Rather than aggressively cutting calories, the diet emphasizes nutrient-dense foods that help you feel full longer. Healthy fats from olive oil, fiber-rich vegetables, and protein from fish help regulate appetite naturally.

  • Fewer blood sugar spikes
